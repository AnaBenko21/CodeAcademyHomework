Things to do in Bali

This paradise on Earth combines stunning scenery, incredible biodiversity, and fascinating architecture.

Top activities

Scale one of Bali’s natural wonders with a private, early-morning hike up Mount Batur, including breakfast and a trip to a coffee plantation afterwards. With all entry tickets, accommodation transfers, and an expert local guide also included, the only thing you’ll have to worry about is enjoying the panoramic views.
